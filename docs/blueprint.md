# **App Name**: BabyCare Time

## Core Features:

- Initial Setup: Input the baby's birth date to calculate initial milk requirements based on WHO guidelines.
- Feeding Schedule Prediction: Predict the next feeding time based on WHO guidelines and historical feeding data.
- Feeding Input: Record feeding data, including time, type (breast milk or formula), and quantity (ml).
- Feeding History: Display a history of feedings, showing time, type, and quantity.
- WHO Guidelines Reference: Reference WHO guidelines when advising the user

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) for a calming and nurturing feel.
- Background color: Pale cream (#FAF9F6), providing a gentle backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#80CBC4) for interactive elements, adding a touch of freshness.
- Font pairing: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text and 'Literata' (serif) for headlines, for clarity and readability.
- Use gentle, rounded icons related to feeding, time, and baby care.
- Clean and intuitive layout with a focus on easy data entry and clear visualizations.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback during feeding data input.
